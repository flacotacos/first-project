The Rookie 1996

Kobe Bryant's rookie season was 1996-97. He was the second-youngest player to ever play in an NBA game at 18 years and 72 days old. He also became the youngest NBA starter at 18 years and 158 days old. Bryant was drafted right after high school by the Charlotte Hornets as their 13th pick during the 1996-1997 NBA draft. He averaged 7.6 points per game on 41% shooting. He mostly came off the bench behind guards Eddie Jones and Nick Van Exel.
